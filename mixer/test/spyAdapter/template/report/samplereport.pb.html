Services

HandleSampleReportService

HandleSampleReportService is implemented by backends that wants to handle request-time ‘samplereport’ instances.

rpc HandleSampleReport(HandleSampleReportRequest) returns (istio.mixer.adapter.model.v1beta1.ReportResult)

HandleSampleReport is called by Mixer at request-time to deliver ‘samplereport’ instances to the backend.
